Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit India for cultural tourism?

October to March is generally the best time, with pleasant weather and many festivals. However, different regions have different peak seasons.

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Most foreign nationals require a visa. India offers e-Visa facilities for tourism purposes for many countries. Check the official Indian visa website for details.

How can I book guided cultural tours?

Contact us through this form or call our helpline. We can connect you with certified tour guides and cultural experts.

Are the heritage sites accessible for people with disabilities?

Many major sites are working on accessibility improvements. Contact us for specific information about accessible travel options.
